Geospatial Analysis

Geospatial analysis is like making a giant puzzle with maps. It helps us understand where things are and how they relate to each other. We can use it to find the best routes to go places or to see patterns in nature, like where animals live or where it might flood. It's like having a superpower to see the world from above and solve mysteries about our planet. Fascinating isn't it?
